About Us

Welcome to OKID Handmade Jewellery Collection,

In the world of fashion and accessories, handmade jewellery has gained a lot of attention for its uniqueness, creativity, and personal touch. Beaded jewellery in particular, is loved for its intricate designs, vibrant colours, and customizability. However, many artisans struggle to find a suitable platform to display and sell their creations. The goal of this project is to develop an online handmade beaded jewellery selling website offering a space for artisans to showcase and for customers to purchase unique, personalized jewellery items. The website will allow users to browse through a variety of handcrafted beaded jewellery such as necklaces, bracelets, earrings, rings and other custom pieces, offering a seamless shopping experience from the comfort of their homes. This platform will also empower independent jewellery makers by giving them the ability to reach a larger, global audience, while creating a space for jewellery enthusiasts to discover and purchase truly unique items.
